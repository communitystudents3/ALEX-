Referencing: APA 7 vs Harvard (Quick Guide)

2025-09-22

In-text citations

  • APA 7: (Author, Year, p. 23)
  • Harvard: Author (Year, p. 23)

Reference list

APA uses sentence case for titles; Harvard often uses similar but varies by style guide. Always check your institution’s guide.
